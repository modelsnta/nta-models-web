Get Scouted

At NTA Models, we take pride in diversity, and wholeheartedly support the global movement for inclusion, providing everyone with the chance to shine regardless of height, age, gender, race, or size.

Submission Guideline:

  1. Models / Talent under age should have guardian or parent consent.
  2. All experienced models should include their portfolio or Instagram for the team to review.
  3. If a model attended our academy, there's no need to apply as we will contact you if we want to represent you.
  4. It takes 5 - 7 working days for us to give you feedback. Whether your application is successful or unsuccessful, we will contact you.
  5. We use Google Drive to see your pictures. Applications without the Google Drive link will not be considered.
  6. To enroll at our academy, visit the ENROLL page.

PHOTO SUBMISSION:

HEADSHOT: Please supply a clear portrait shot of your face and shoulders (no sunglasses or hats) and please do not wear any make up.

PROFILE: Either side of your face.

WAIST UP: Please try take this against a plain background.

FULL LENGTH: Please try take this against a plain background.

GOOGLE DRIVE: Upload your photos to Google Drive and share the link. Paste it along with your application Or email them to models@ntamodels.co.za
