Introducing our NTA Character

The NTA Character division is the powerful, authentic face of modern Commercial modeling. We are not defined by standardized beauty, but by the undeniable strength of individual truth. This division is dedicated to representing models and talent whose unique look, compelling story, and distinct personality are their greatest assets. We champion the outliers, the memorable faces, and the industry professionals who bring genuine depth and relatability to brands. If you possess a look that challenges convention and speaks volumes, you belong with Character. This is commercial modeling, redefined by Identity, Individuality, and Diversity (IID).

IID

identity, individuality & diversity

NTA Character stands for IID because we believe the future of brand storytelling demands authenticity. We actively seek out the rich spectrum of human experience to create campaigns that truly resonate. Our commitment to Identity, Individuality, and Diversity is our foundation, ensuring every voice is seen and heard.

Identity:

Identity is the core belief that your background, history, and life experiences are an irreplaceable part of your market value. We look for models who are grounded in their self-perception, unafraid to own their narrative. In the modeling and fashion industry, this shift changes the narrative from aspiration to relatability. It moves commercial advertising from generic perfection to specific, human connection, making campaigns stronger and more effective by featuring people viewers can genuinely see themselves in.

Individuality:

Individuality is the celebration of what makes one person utterly unique—whether it's a gap tooth, unique scar, striking hair texture, or an atypical silhouette. NTA Character seeks the compelling details that define you outside of conventional standards. This is important because individual characteristics create instant memorability in an oversaturated market. Instead of blending in, our talent stand out, offering brands a distinctive edge and proving that originality drives attention and engagement.

Diversity:

Diversity at NTA is a comprehensive commitment that encompasses race, ethnicity, age, size, ability, and gender presentation. Our vision is to ensure our roster authentically reflects the global marketplace. We actively seek out talent from all walks of life, recognizing that a truly diverse selection of faces and voices is not a trend, but a necessity for modern commerce. This approach drives global relevance for our clients, creating a powerful, ethical ecosystem that uplifts communities and connects brands with every demographic.
