The Academy Division

The NTA Academy is South Africa's leading model and talent development institution. We go beyond basic posing and runway training to offer a holistic curriculum that prepares aspiring artists for the real-world demands of the industry. Our program is meticulously designed to cultivate not just professional skills, but also the confidence, discipline, and strong character necessary for long-term success. We focus on areas like media training, financial literacy for artists, digital branding, and international industry standards. When you join the NTA Academy, you're not just taking classes—you're investing in a career foundation built on excellence and ethical practice.
