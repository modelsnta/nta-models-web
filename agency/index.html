The Standard of NTA Agency

NTA Agency represents the pinnacle of modern modeling, focusing on Editorial and High Fashion placements across global markets. We nurture and manage talent with distinct runway appeal, compelling photographic presence, and the professional discipline required by top-tier designers and publications. Our models are the faces defining the next generation of fashion, embodying sophistication, versatility, and influence. From Mainboard professionals to promising Development talent, we ensure our models are prepared to excel on any international stage.

The future of fashion starts here

Our Agency divisions are meticulously organized to ensure specialized management and focused career growth for every model, regardless of their stage in the industry.

Women & Men:

Mainboard & Development

Our Mainboard is home to established professionals working consistently in high fashion and major commercial campaigns. Development focuses on nurturing new talent, providing comprehensive training, portfolio development, and market preparation to transition them successfully onto the main roster.

Novice:

First Steps into Professional Modeling

The Novice division is dedicated to individuals just beginning their journey. We offer guidance on basic industry standards, scouting opportunities, and foundational confidence building, bridging the gap between amateur interest and serious career pursuit.

Youth:

Future Generations of Talent

The Youth division manages models under the age of 18, ensuring professional work is balanced with legal and educational requirements. We focus on building a safe, supportive environment for young talent to gain experience in commercial and fashion sectors.
