ABOUT

Established in 2020 in Bloemfontein, Free State, NTA Models has established a prestigious reputation as a leading model agency and academy.

NTA Models started as a model and talent academy based in Free State. We successfully run the academy for 5 years with the goal of educating, empowering and shaping the future of our aspiring models and talent in South Africa. Our commitment to building our talent is reflected in the high-profile brands we have collaborated with in Bloemfontein. We have worked closely with recognized names such as Sadè Jansen Fashion Designer, Bently's Men Boutique, and BMW Bloemfontein. The academy grew and more talent was being developed and trained exceptionally well.


In 2025, NTA Models upgraded to a new venture, introducing NTA Models as a full modeling agency plus academy. This approach ensures students we have invested time in developing are provided a bigger opportunity to be represented by us.


With the new approach of being a modeling agency plus academy, our goal is to educate, develop, nurture and empower our models, developing a new generation of aspirational and formidable talent.


Our team is dedicated to working strategically to forecast and manage each talent's career. We believe in cultivating a mindful and healthy working environment for every model, colleague, and client under our care.

Tlotlisang

Founder & CEO of NTA Models. Tlotlisang started the agency with the goal to dismantle traditional modeling by introducing the power of Individuality, Identity, and Diversity. With his experience and leadership, he has run the agency for 5 years, gaining collaborations with big brands and redefining beauty standards and empowering the aspiring models.

The Integrated NTA Path

Our dual structure—Agency and Academy—provides a holistic path for talent development. The NTA Academy focuses on foundational mastery: professional posing, runway technique, communication skills, and financial literacy.

Once a model is signed to the NTA Agency, management shifts to career strategy, international placement, brand building, and negotiating contracts. This integrated approach ensures that our models are not just aesthetically ready, but are also professional, informed, and equipped to navigate the complexities of a demanding global industry with confidence.

The FAIRe Movement

We stand firmly and support FAIRe.

FAIRe is a foundation and movement created to protect the rights, identities, and livelihoods of real human models in an industry increasingly threatened by artificial intelligence.

Commitment to the POPI Act

The Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPI Act) is the cornerstone of data privacy in South Africa. At NTA Models SA, we are fully committed to upholding the POPI Act.

This means we treat the personal information (including portfolio images, contact details, and contractual data) of our models, clients, and partners with the utmost care, ensuring lawful processing, secured storage, and transparent use. We only collect data necessary for the performance of our services, and we ensure you always have the right to access and correct your personal information held by us.


Should you feel aggrieved with the manner in which your personal information is processed you may also lodge a complaint to the Information regulator. Email: complaints.IR@justice.gov.za or General enquires email: inforeg@justice.gov.za
